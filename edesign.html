Get Your eDesign Here

eDesign is quickly becoming the most popular way to design a room. It's affordable and gives you the opportunity to design your room with a designers assistance. Here's how it works !

Tell us about the space that you would like designed. When you call, we will discuss your style, needs, space and budget. You send pics and measurements of the space you would like designed and then work one on one with a virtual designer to choose your layout and furnishings. We design 3 vision boards and you select the design that you prefer. All information you need to design your room will be made available to you with a shopping list and links for easy ordering. You can just click, order and have everything delivered right to your home. A floor plan, and overview of the design with specific instructions for furniture placement is included in the design plan. Unlimited revisions are available if necessary so that your designed space is perfect and exactly what you want. A designer will be available for consults throughout the project using FaceTime, Skype, text, call or email.
